Nested Lists

We can place list inside another list to create a sublist.

  1. First Period: 8:20-9:48
  2. Second Period: 9:55-11:27
  3. Block Lunch:
    1. A Block: 11:27-11:57
    2. B Block: 11:57-12:27
  4. Third Period: 12:32-2:00
  5. Fourth Period: 2:07-3:35
